Let me Introduce about this project

The project focused on understanding the unique health challenges and behaviors of urban populations, including lifestyle-related diseases and stress-related health issues. Through user research to design to usability testing, the project sought to create a user-friendly digital platform that encourages regular health check-ups, provides personalized health recommendations, and integrates seamlessly with existing healthcare systems. The design emphasized ease of use, cultural relevance, and trust-building to ensure broad adoption and sustained use of preventive health measures.

Why did I choose this project?

Industry Growth

Amidst the burgeoning healthcare landscape in urban India, we found ourselves drawn to the preventive healthcare sector. Witnessing the exponential growth of this industry, driven by an increasing awareness of health issues and a growing middle class, we saw immense potential for impactful interventions.

Lack of User-Centered Products

However, we observed a glaring gap in the market – a lack of user-centered products. Existing solutions often failed to resonate with individuals on a personal level, lacking the customization and empathy needed to drive meaningful behavior change.

Emotional Connection

Driven by a passion for making a difference in people's lives, we sought to forge an emotional connection between individuals and their health. Understanding that healthcare decisions are deeply intertwined with emotions, we aimed to create solutions that would not only address physical ailments but also resonate with individuals on a personal level, inspiring them to take proactive steps towards wellness.

Business Opportunity

Recognizing the pressing need to shift from reactive to preventive healthcare, we saw a significant business opportunity. By leveraging technology, data analytics, and personalized approaches, we envisioned creating a scalable business model that would not only improve health outcomes but also drive economic value.

User Problems, Solution Point & Wireframe

User Problem 1

User Problem 2

Motivation and Engagement with Fitness Regimens

I have observed that interest and motivation towards fitness vary. Walking is often highlighted as an accessible activity, but I acknowledge the challenges in sticking to structured fitness routines.

Solution Point

To address fluctuating motivation, the app can send motivational reminders and messages to users. It can also include a rewards system where users earn points or badges for achieving their fitness goals, which can be a great motivator.

Navigate to Mental Health Services

The user navigates to the mental health services section of the app. This could be done through a main menu or a dashboard.

Choose a Professional

The user is presented with a list of mental health professionals available for consultation. They can view each professional’s profile, which includes their qualifications, areas of expertise, and availability.

Schedule a Consultation

The user selects a professional and schedules a consultation at a time that suits their schedule. The app should offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate the user’s time constraints.

Attend the Consultation

At the scheduled time, the user attends the virtual consultation via a video call within the app. This allows them to engage with mental health services from the comfort of their own home or any other convenient location.

Follow-up and Continued Care

After the consultation, the user can schedule follow-up appointments, access resources recommended by the professional, and track their progress over time. This encourages continued engagement with mental health services.

Setting Fitness Goals

The user sets their fitness goals in the app. These could be steps walked, calories burned, hours of physical activity, etc.

Enabling Motivational Reminders

The user enables the “Motivational Reminders and Rewards” feature in the settings of the Mobile App.

Customizing Reminders

The user customizes the reminders according to their preference. They can set the frequency, time, and type of motivational messages they want to receive.

Engaging with the App

As the user engages with the fitness regimen, they receive motivational reminders and messages at the set times. These reminders encourage the user to stick to their fitness goals.

Achieving Goals & Earning Rewards

When the user achieves their fitness goals, they earn points or badges in the app. These rewards serve as a motivation for the user to continue their fitness regimen.

Tracking Progress

The user can track their progress in the “Progress” section of the Mobile App. This includes the goals achieved, points earned, and badges won.

I choose Light Mode for my Visual Design. But why?

Readability & Visibility

Better Contrast: Light mode typically offers higher contrast for text, making it easier to read for most users. This is particularly important in healthcare apps where clear and readable information can directly impact user comprehension and decision-making.


Ambient Light: Light mode performs well in various lighting conditions, especially in bright environments where dark mode might cause glare or make content difficult to see.

Psychological & Emotional Impact

Positive Associations: Light mode can create a clean, fresh, and professional look, which aligns well with the trust and reliability expected from healthcare services. It can evoke a sense of openness and clarity, essential for users dealing with health-related information.


Energy and Alertness: Light backgrounds can make users feel more alert and focused, which is beneficial when they are navigating through important health information or making decisions about their preventive care.

User Demographics

Accessibility: Light mode is generally more accessible to a broader audience, including elderly users who might have vision impairments. The higher contrast and brightness can aid those with low vision or other visual challenges.


User Preference: Studies have shown that a significant portion of users prefer light mode for applications they use during the day, which is when most users would engage with a preventive healthcare app.

Content Presentation

Medical Imagery and Graphs: Medical images, graphs, and charts often look more natural and are easier to interpret against a light background. This is crucial in preventive healthcare apps where users might be reviewing their health metrics or visualizing health trends.


Form and Input Fields: Forms and input fields are often easier to fill out and distinguish in light mode, reducing user errors and improving the overall user experience.

Battery Consumption

Neutral Battery Impact: While dark mode is often praised for its battery-saving capabilities, light mode does not significantly drain battery life on most modern devices with LCD screens. Given that many users might use the app for short, frequent sessions, the difference in battery consumption may be negligible.

Consistency & Standards

Design Consistency: Many established healthcare apps and websites use light mode, making it a familiar environment for users. This consistency can reduce the learning curve and improve usability.


Industry Standards: Following industry standards in healthcare app design can enhance credibility and user trust, as users expect a certain level of professionalism and standardization in healthcare-related applications.

Set Fitness Goals

The screen allows users to set and save their fitness goals, such as daily steps, calories burned, and activity hours. This organized layout helps users track their progress and stay motivated, leading to better health outcomes through consistent monitoring and goal-setting.

Motivational Reminders

The screen allows users to customize motivational reminders to suit their personal needs and preferences.
This personalization can help users stay motivated and productive by receiving tailored reminders. The “Save Settings” button ensures that these preferences are saved, providing consistent reinforcement of motivational content.

Customize Reminders

The screen allows users to customize and save daily reminders for topics like quotes and health tips at their preferred times. This helps users stay organized and motivated by receiving timely, personalized reminders without needing to set them manually each time.

Achievement

The pop-up content on the screen provides immediate positive reinforcement by congratulating users on reaching their daily steps goal. This can motivate users to stay engaged with the app and maintain healthy habits. The “Close” and “View Progress” buttons offer simple options for users to either return to the main dashboard or review their activity data, enhancing user interaction and personal health monitoring.

Progress Tracker

This screen provides users with a clear and organized overview of their fitness progress. It includes motivational achievement badges, a graph to visualize daily activity levels, and icons for different exercises with corresponding times. Additionally, it offers options to share progress and update goals, helping users stay accountable and adjust their fitness objectives as needed.

Mental Health Services

The screen provides users with tools to manage their mental health effectively. The Current Goals feature tracks progress in physical activities like jogging and swimming, promoting a sense of accomplishment. Additionally, the dashboard offers a clear overview of daily achievements and encourages users to prioritize their mental health.

Professional Selection

The screen provides a convenient way for users to find and schedule appointments with professionals. It displays names, profile pictures, ratings, and available times, allowing users to make informed decisions quickly and efficiently.

Filters

The screen is functionally beneficial for users by offering a streamlined way to find and schedule appointments with healthcare specialists. Users can easily navigate through various medical specialties, select convenient appointment times, and filter practitioners based on ratings, ensuring they receive quality care efficiently.

Appointment Booking

The screen provides a clear and user-friendly interface for booking appointments with a healthcare professional. Users can easily view available dates and times, select their preferred slots, and access additional information about the doctor and patient reviews. This makes the process of scheduling appointments convenient and efficient.

Virtual Consultation

The screen provides users with a convenient platform for real-time consultations with healthcare professionals. It allows for immediate communication through video calls, which can be more reassuring and informative than text or voice calls alone. Additionally, the text conversation feature supports ongoing care management by tracking patient progress and concerns, making healthcare more personalized and responsive to individual needs.

Consultation Summary

The screen provides users with easy access to essential health resources and information, aiding in managing health. It allows users to conveniently schedule follow-up appointments with a doctor and centralizes educational materials related to health, empowering users to take proactive steps in their healthcare journey.

Key Aspects considered for User Interface Design

Golden Ratio

The golden ratio is evident in the layout, guiding the user’s eye naturally through the content. The spiral overlays and grid lines show how elements like the “Dashboard,” “Features,” and “Current Goals” sections are proportioned to create a harmonious and aesthetically pleasing design. This mathematical principle is known to resonate with users subconsciously, making the interface feel more balanced and engaging.

Gestalt Principles

Proximity

Elements such as “Intelligent Time Manager” and “Personalized Health Plan” are grouped closely, indicating they are related functions. This helps users quickly understand the relationship between different features.

Similarity

Consistent use of colors and shapes, like the circular progress bars for “Current Goals,” signals that these elements belong to the same category. This visual consistency aids in quicker recognition and understanding.

Continuity

The curved lines of the golden ratio spiral guide the user’s gaze smoothly across the interface, from the top “Dashboard” section to the “Recent Achievements” at the bottom. This continuous flow reduces cognitive load and enhances navigation.

Closure

Incomplete visual elements, such as the progress bars, are perceived as whole due to the principle of closure. This helps users quickly grasp the information without needing complete visual cues.

UX Laws

Law of Prägnanz (Simplicity)

The design is simple and clean, using clear icons and minimal text. This simplicity ensures that users can quickly understand and interact with the app without feeling overwhelmed.

Fitts’s Law

Interactive elements like buttons are appropriately sized for easy tapping, minimizing physical effort and enhancing usability. This is crucial for mobile interfaces where touch accuracy can vary.

Hick’s Law

By grouping options under categories like “Features” and “Current Goals,” the design reduces choice overload and simplifies decision-making. Users can find what they need more efficiently, improving overall user experience.

User Cognition, Adoption and Alignment

The integration of the golden ratio, Gestalt Principles, and UX Laws in this design facilitates quick cognition adoption by aligning with natural human perception patterns. The harmonious proportions, clear grouping, and intuitive navigation make interactions feel instinctive, reducing mental effort and enhancing user satisfaction. This alignment ensures that users can easily adopt and align with the app’s functionalities, leading to a more seamless and enjoyable experience.

By leveraging these design principles, the app not only looks aesthetically pleasing but also functions in a way that aligns with how users naturally process information, making it both beautiful and highly usable.

User Name:

Satisfaction

The screen has a sleek, modern look that’s easy on the eyes. Finding my achievements, points, and badges is effortless, thanks to the intuitive layout. The trophies, badges, and points add a fun, rewarding touch, making progress feel exciting. Plus, every activity is clearly listed with its time and date, so I always know exactly where I stand.

Acceptance

The screen is simple and easy to navigate, making it accessible even for those who aren't tech-savvy. The features align well with my health and fitness goals, and it's great to have all this essential information in one place. Plus, being able to share progress and update goals directly from this screen keeps me more engaged and motivated to stay on track.

Improvement Areas

The graph showing points earned could be clearer with a legend or more context, making it easier to interpret at a glance. It would be great to have options for customizing the types of goals and activities displayed based on personal preferences and routines. Adding notifications or reminders for reaching goals and earning points could make the experience even more engaging.
